External compressible flow study

Baseline case plus angle-of-attack and Mach sweeps for a trapezoidal wing in Ansys, with reporting in body and wind axes.

Open PDF Back to projects

Reference values

  • Planform area reported as 0.75345 m².
  • Mean aerodynamic chord computed from root/tip chords (reported result 0.6462 m).

Force transform

Converted forces from the world/wing reference frame into wind axes via a 2D rotation using angle of attack.

Studies

  • Baseline at Mach 0.8395 and AoA 3.06°.
  • AoA sweep reporting CL, CD, and CM across multiple angles.
  • Mach sweep at constant AoA 3.06° with changes in lift/drag breakdown.

What the results show

As AoA increases, lift rises while drag grows rapidly and becomes pressure-drag dominated; pitching moment trends more negative with AoA in the reported summary.

Deliverables

  • Characteristic curves: CL vs AoA, CM vs AoA, CL vs CD, plus Mach sweeps.
  • Contour plots: pressure, Mach number, y+ distribution, velocity components, shear stress.

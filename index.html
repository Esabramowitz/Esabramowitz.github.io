Eytan Abramowitz

I’m Eytan Abramowitz, a mechanical engineering student at Columbia University focused on aerospace, simulation, and hands-on design.

My work centers on using analysis to guide real engineering decisions, especially in aerodynamics, structures, and product development. Through Columbia’s AIAA Design Build Fly team, I’ve led a 10-member aerodynamics team and helped manage aircraft design, simulation, and manufacturing efforts from concept through fabrication.

I’ve also conducted CFD and structural studies for flight systems and worked on robotics and experimental aerodynamics projects in both lab and research settings. I’m especially interested in projects that combine modeling, testing, fabrication, and leadership to turn ideas into reliable, high-performance systems.
